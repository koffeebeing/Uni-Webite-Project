Home Students

As an undergraduate student, you may be able to apply for a loan to cover the cost of your tuition fees, which will be paid directly to MBO.
There are no up-front fees required.
Repayment only starts after you finish your course and are earning over £26,575.
If you haven't finished repaying your loan after 30 years it will be automatically cancelled.

To apply visit the Student Finance website.

Maintenance loan

You may also be able to apply for a loan to help with living costs such as food, travel and accommodation. To apply visit the Student Finance website.

Supplementary grants

The government also offers the following additional support:

  • Parents' Learning Allowance
  • Adult Dependants' Grant
  • Childcare Grant
  • Disabled Students Allowance
All nursing and many allied health students on courses from September 2020 receive a payment of at least £5,000 a year from the government.

For more information, visit the Student Finance website.
